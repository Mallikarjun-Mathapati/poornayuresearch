Welcome to PRL

We have sufficient analytical tools to fulfill potential needs of the pharmaceutical customers.

Analytical testing procedures or quality control tests are the essential requirement as a regulatory compliance for pharmaceuticals. Here at Poornayu, we help the customers with wide range of integrated scientific analytical services as per the published guidelines of standard pharmacopoeias (IP, USP, EP etc). We support the pharmaceutical manufacturers in providing the quality test results related to raw materials, excipients, active Pharm Ingredients (APIs), Bulk drugs, intermediate compounds and finished products. Poornayu extensively dedicated to the quality control and analytical development activities.

Vision

Engage with global pharmaceutical clients for improving the health and quality of life.

Mission

Integrate and implement the quality of Pharma testing with utmost customer service.

Our Priority

Analytical testing techniques, often known as quality control testing, are the commonest parameter for product quality and acceptance. At Poornayu, we provide our clients with a comprehensive variety of integrated scientific & analytical services in accordance to customer compliance. We assist our clients in supplying quality test results for various organic, natural and chemical products received from various customers. Our focus is on analytical method development, validation and quantification activities for the research related compounds.

We have acquired analytical instruments that can cater to the needs of our research oriented clients across various industries. The reporting and documentation procedure is solely based on real and authentic testing methods. We deliver dependable and high-quality service support that leads to credibility. This has increased PRL for unwavering commitment to deadlines and high-quality technical services.

At the Analytical Lab, we offer a comprehensive suite of analytical services, including chemical, physical, and biological analyses.
