Dr. Arunkumar
M.Pharm, Ph.D

Technical Professional having 10+ years of experience in formulation research and analytical studies in Pharma and Personal Care Products sector. Has experience across new product development like hydrogels/ Aquagels, chewable tablets, Oro-dispersible tablets and Gastro retentive dosage forms (solid and liquid dosage forms), soft gelatin capsules, medicated toothpastes (Aqueous/ Non-aqueous) and mouthwashes. Technical expertise in handling instruments such as HPLC, UV- spectrophotometer, Rheometer, particle size analyzer, Scanning electron microscopy (SEM). Skilled in operating equipments such as compaction simulator (EHS-UK), Spray dryer, transdermal diffusion assembly, tablet coater and blender. Preparation of documents that includes stability study updates as per the ICH-GCP guidelines / as per the customer demand, capable of preparing technical service reports (TSR’s), SOP’s and technical presentations. Published 4 international research papers and 5 posters (AAPS-U.S.A).
